Delima Husada Gresik is the name of an educational foundation and a health analyst academy located in Gresik, East Java. Delima Husada Gresik Education Foundation was established in 1997 and is under the Ministry of National Education. Akademi Analis Kesehatan Delima Husada Gresik (AAK Delima Husada) is one of the study programs organized by the foundation, which offers D3 education in the field of health analyst. AAK Delima Husada has received accreditation B from LAMPTKES in 2016. AAK Delima Husada has seven lecturers and 153 active students. AAK Delima Husada aims to produce qualified and professional graduates in the field of health laboratory.
